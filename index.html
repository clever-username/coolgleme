Coit Comedy - Branding, Marketing, Production

https://www.coitcomedy.com/

March 2019 - February 2020

Coit Comedy was a monthly comedy showcase in the basement of historic Columbus Cafe and featured some of the best comedians from around the Bay Area and beyond as well as free Golden Boy Pizza. This show ran for about a year before COVID made laughing and sharing pizza in a crowded basement a terrifying prospect. When I conceptualized this show, I was living and working in North Beach, San Francisco and I wanted the artwork and the show itself to bear the mark of the neighborhood. I achieved this by incorporating Coit Tower into the logo and modeled the flyer after the Fernet posters that are ubiquitous in the area. I also gave away two "sheets" of Golden Boy Pizza, a local favorite, at every show. My co-host Cole Chapman and I would book a lineup and host the show each month, and I would create marketing materials for Instagram and Mailchimp campaigns to drive recurring and new audience alike. We hope to continue as soon as local mandates allow.


Copywriting - Chubby Noodle Saké Menu Descriptions

Chubby-Noodle-Sake-Menu-Descriptions.jpg

February 2019

As a Lead Bartender at Chubby Noodle in North Beach San Francisco, I helped create a bar program featuring a variety of craft sakés and refreshing drinks. In addition I was tasked with writing playful copy for the menu that would match the lively, casual atmosphere that the restaurant is known for while accurately describing the unique tasting notes of each saké. The specific challenges of writing amusing yet detailed descriptions in the very few characters afforded by the menu made this a particularly fun project.
